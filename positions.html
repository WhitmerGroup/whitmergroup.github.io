Postdoctoral Openings

The Whitmer Group seeks applications from interested postdoctoral scholars to perform advanced sampling calculations on self-assembled and polymer materials. Contact Prof. Whitmer if you have questions.

Graduate and Undergraduate Projects

The Whitmer group is looking for capable, energetic graduate and undergraduate students. Current open projects involve the development of free energy mapping methods, liquid crystal property computation, drug delivery complexes, and many other interesting topics in soft materials, thermodynamics and computational methods. Please contact Prof. Whitmer for more information.

Undergraduate Summer Opportunities

Undergraduate students from outside Notre Dame interested in spending a summer working with the Whitmer group are encouraged to apply through the NURF, PODEMOS, or iSURE programs.
